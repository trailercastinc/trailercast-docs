TrailerCast is a meeting recording tool with AI analysis built in, plus an AI Sales Experience Platform layered on top. It does four things:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Record + analyze every call" icon="microphone">
    Joins your Zoom / Google Meet / Teams as a participant, captures the recording, transcribes it speaker-by-speaker, and produces a structured AI summary: key takeaways, topics, talk-time breakdown, objections raised, action items, qualification verdict. Every call becomes a searchable artifact your team can review in 60 seconds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Turn demos into trailers" icon="film">
    The AI picks the moments that matter, features being demonstrated, prospect reactions, value props, and stitches them into a 10–15 minute highlight trailer. Long enough to carry context, short enough that prospects finish them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track prospect engagement" icon="chart-line">
    See exactly which moments prospects rewatched, which they skipped, and where they bounced. Stop guessing whether your follow-up landed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build Buyer Spaces" icon="rectangle-list">
    Interactive Decision Rooms combining your trailer with discovery, comparison, ROI, and a live conversation thread between you and the prospect's evaluation team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

If you sell B2B and you spend any time creating follow-up materials for prospects after a discovery call or demo, TrailerCast replaces 80% of that work with an AI that does it in 3 minutes per recording.
