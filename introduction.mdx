TrailerCast is an AI Sales Experience Platform. It does three things:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Turn calls into trailers" icon="film">
    Upload a demo recording and the AI picks the moments that matter, features being demonstrated, prospect reactions, value props, and stitches them into a 60–180s trailer.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track prospect engagement" icon="chart-line">
    See exactly which moments prospects rewatched, which they skipped, and where they bounced. Stop guessing whether your follow-up landed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build Buyer Spaces" icon="rectangle-list">
    Interactive Decision Rooms combining your trailer with discovery, comparison, ROI, and a live conversation thread between you and the prospect's team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What TrailerCast is *not*

- A meeting recorder (we integrate with Recall.ai but don't compete with Gong on call libraries)
- A content management system or pitch-deck builder
- Generic video editing software

We do one thing, turn long sales calls into short, prospect-ready trailers with engagement tracking. If you sell B2B and spend any time on prospect follow-up after a discovery call or demo, TrailerCast replaces 80% of that work with an AI that does it in 3 minutes per recording.
