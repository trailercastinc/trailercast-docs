The **Reports** phase has three tabs:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Top Demos">
    Ranked by engagement (views × completion × rewatches). Click any row to open the demo.

    Useful for:
    - Identifying your team's hits, what cuts work?
    - Finding under-shared demos with high engagement (someone made gold but only sent it to one person)
    - Coaching: "watch this trailer, see how it opens?"
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="By User">
    Per-rep breakdown:
    - Demos created
    - Total views generated
    - Avg engagement per demo
    - Won / lost / pending deals (when CRM integration is connected)

    Sortable by every column. Personal scope (just you) vs Team scope (everyone) toggle at the top.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Activity">
    Chronological feed of every play, share, comment, follow-up across the team. Useful for:
    - Daily standup: "what happened with prospects yesterday?"
    - Audit trail of recent activity
    - Spotting when a stalled deal re-engages
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Personal vs Team scope

The toggle at the top of the Reports phase switches between:

| Scope | What you see |
|---|---|
| **Personal** | Just your demos and your prospects |
| **Team** | Everyone's demos and prospects |

Team scope is gated to **Admin and Global Admin**. Members see Personal-only.

## Common reporting workflows

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Friday end-of-week review">
    1. Reports → Activity tab
    2. Filter to "this week"
    3. Skim what happened across the team
    4. Note any stalled threads to follow up Monday
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Weekly forecast prep">
    1. Reports → By User → your row
    2. Check pending deal count
    3. Cross-reference with Conversations tab, which threads are warm?
    4. Walk into your forecast call with clean data
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Coaching session prep">
    1. Reports → Top Demos
    2. Filter to a specific rep
    3. Pick their highest-engagement demo
    4. Watch it together; note what they did right
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quarter retrospective">
    1. Demos-per-quarter sparkline on dashboard
    2. Reports → By User to compare reps
    3. Activity tab for qualitative signal
    4. Inform Q+1 goals
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What's NOT in Reports

Today (Q2 2026):

- **Deal forecasting**, we surface engagement signal but don't predict deal close
- **Custom dashboards**, fixed three-tab structure for now
- **Exporting to BI tools**, coming via API access in Pro+ tier
