## Sign up

Go to [trailercast.cloud/signup](https://trailercast.cloud/signup).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1. Email and password">
    Enter your work email and choose a password. The strength meter shows red → yellow → green; aim for green. Click **Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2. Company and role">
    Enter your company name, your full name, and your role. Click **Create account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on the dashboard">
    You're auto-assigned the **Global Admin** role for your company since you're the first user. You can transfer this later if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If your company already has a TrailerCast account and a teammate sent you an invite, click the link in their email instead of signing up directly, it routes you into their workspace and skips the company-creation step.
</Tip>

## Your trial

You start on a **7-day free trial** with full feature access. No credit card required.

- All paid-plan features unlocked during the trial
- A small banner at the top shows days remaining
- At the end of the trial you'll be prompted to pick a plan; nothing auto-bills

See [Billing](/billing) for plan details.
