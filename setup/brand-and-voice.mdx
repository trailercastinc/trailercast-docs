## Brand settings

<Note>
  Find it at: **Settings → Brand**
</Note>

Three things to set:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your logo" icon="image">
    PNG, ideally 1024×1024 or larger, transparent background. Used on:
    - The share-page header your prospects see
    - Trailer end cards
    - As a watermark on free-tier trailers
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your primary color" icon="paintbrush">
    Hex code (e.g. `#0891b2`). Used as the accent color on share pages and Buyer Spaces.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set company name display" icon="building">
    How it appears on share pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

These settings make every trailer feel like part of *your* brand, not a generic template.

## Voice cloning

<Note>
  Find it at: **Settings → Voice**. Available on Pro tier and above.
</Note>

If you'd like trailer narration in your own voice (or your CEO's, with their permission), record a 10–30 second voice sample and upload it. The AI uses it as the narration voice on every trailer your account produces.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Record a sample">
    Read 10–30 seconds of any text in a quiet room. The AI can clone from a short sample but more is better, aim for 20s minimum.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload">
    MP3 or WAV. Click **Upload voice sample**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait ~30 seconds">
    The voice gets registered with our voice provider. You'll see "Voice cloned ✓" when it's ready.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test it">
    Process a demo. The trailer's narration uses your cloned voice automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Don't want voice narration at all? Set narration to **off** in your trailer preferences. Trailers ship with original audio only.
</Tip>

## Catalogues

Pro+ tiers also include:
- **Voice catalogue**, pre-recorded professional voices to choose from if you don't want to clone
- **Language catalogue**, narration in 20+ languages

Both available in **Settings → Voice**.
